Product pins hold specific products at fixed positions in a collection. A pinned product is never moved by a sort or sync — it stays exactly where you placed it.

There are two types:

- **Top pins** — products held at positions 1, 2, 3, … at the start of the collection
- **Bottom pins** — products held at the last positions, at the end of the collection

![Pin management panel showing two top-pinned products and one bottom-pinned product with drag handles](/screenshots/placeholder.png)

## Pins require membership

:::caution
**A pin controls position, not membership.**

A product must already be a member of the collection — matched by your rules or included via the baseline — before a pin takes effect. Pinning a product that is not in the collection has no effect.
:::

If you pin a product that is currently not in the collection:

- The pin is saved but shows as **inactive** in the pin list
- The product remains absent from the collection
- If the product later matches your rules and enters the collection, the pin activates automatically and places it at its configured position

This is by design: pins express "where should this product appear", not "force this product in". The rule engine and baseline determine membership; pins determine position within that membership.

:::tip
If a product you want to pin is not matching your rules, first check the live product count in the rule builder with that product in mind. You may need to adjust your conditions, or the product may need a tag or attribute change in Shopify.
:::

## How the sort stack works with pins

When Smart Collections sorts a collection, it applies layers in this order:

1. **Top pins** — placed at positions 1, 2, 3, … in the order you configured
2. **Sort key + demotion** — applied to all un-pinned products
3. **Bottom pins** — placed at the last positions in the order you configured

Pins are absolute — no sort key, demotion rule, or sync can displace them. Out-of-stock demotion applies only to un-pinned products.

**Example:**

```text
Position 1:   [TOP PIN] Featured New Launch
Position 2:   [TOP PIN] Promoted Bundle
Position 3:   Best seller (sorted)
Position 4:   Next product (sorted)
...
Position N-1: [BOTTOM PIN] Discontinued Item
Position N:   [BOTTOM PIN] Clearance Product
```

## Managing pins

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="Add a top pin">

<Steps>

1. Open the collection in Smart Collections and go to the sort settings panel
2. Under **Top pins**, click **Add product**
3. Search for the product by name and select it
4. Drag the pin to set its position relative to other top pins (if you have more than one)
5. Click **Save sort**

</Steps>

The pin takes effect on the next sync. Click **Sync now** to apply it immediately.

  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Add a bottom pin">

<Steps>

1. Open the collection in Smart Collections and go to the sort settings panel
2. Under **Bottom pins**, click **Add product**
3. Search for the product by name and select it
4. Drag the pin to set its position relative to other bottom pins
5. Click **Save sort**

</Steps>

  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Reorder pins">

If you have multiple pins in either group, drag them within the list to change the order. Position numbers update to reflect the new arrangement.

Click **Save sort** to apply the new order. The next sync (or a manual **Sync now**) applies the change to Shopify.

  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Remove a pin">

<Steps>

1. Open the sort settings panel
2. Find the pin in **Top pins** or **Bottom pins**
3. Click the remove icon next to it
4. Click **Save sort**

</Steps>

After removal, the product re-enters the sorted pool on the next sync and is placed according to the sort configuration.

  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Pin limits

Each collection supports up to **100 top pins** and **100 bottom pins**. Reaching the limit for one type does not affect the other.

## Inactive pins

A pin is **inactive** when its product is not currently a member of the collection. Inactive pins:

- Are displayed with a visual indicator in the pin list so you can identify them
- Do not affect the collection's sort order
- Activate automatically if the product later enters the collection

You can leave inactive pins in place or remove them — whichever suits your workflow.

## Pins and out-of-stock demotion

Out-of-stock demotion pushes zero-inventory products to the end of the sorted pool. Pinned products are exempt — a top-pinned product stays at its pinned position even when out of stock.

To allow a pinned product to be demoted when out of stock, remove its pin first.

## Pins and rule evaluation

If a pinned product no longer matches the collection's rules, Smart Collections removes it from the collection on the next sync — the pin has no effect while the product is not a member.

If the product later re-matches the rules, it re-enters the collection and its pin is restored automatically.

:::tip
You can combine pins with any sort key. For example: pin your hero product at position 1, use **Price: Low to High** for everything else. Products fill positions 2 onwards in price order; the pinned product stays at the top regardless of price.
:::
