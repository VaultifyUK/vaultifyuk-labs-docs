This guide walks you through installing Smart Collections and setting up your first managed collection.

## Prerequisites

- Admin access to your Shopify store
- At least one collection to manage (existing or new)

## Installation and setup

<Steps>

1. **Install the app**

   Install Smart Collections from the Shopify App Store.

   ![Smart Collections listing in the Shopify App Store with Install button visible](/screenshots/placeholder.png)

   Click **Add app**, review the permissions requested, then click **Install app**. You will be redirected to the Smart Collections dashboard.

   Smart Collections requests the **Products** permission to read your product catalogue and manage collection membership and sort order. No theme access or customer data is required. GDPR compliance webhooks are registered as required by Shopify; the app stores no customer personal data.

2. **Explore the dashboard**

   The dashboard shows a summary of this month's automation activity and lists all collections currently managed by Smart Collections.

   ![Smart Collections dashboard with summary cards showing this month's automation activity](/screenshots/placeholder.png)

   When you first install, no collections are managed yet. The activity cards populate as you connect collections and syncs begin running.

3. **Create or take over a collection**

   Click **New Collection** to begin. You have two options:

   ![New collection screen showing the option to create new or take over an existing collection](/screenshots/placeholder.png)

   **Create a new collection**

   Smart Collections creates a new Shopify collection and manages it from the start. After saving your rules, it syncs immediately.

   **Take over an existing collection**

   Smart Collections takes over a manual collection already in your store. You choose how:

   | Mode | What it does |
   | --- | --- |
   | **Adopt** (recommended) | Existing products are preserved. The first sync makes **zero changes** to your storefront. Configure rules at your own pace. |
   | **Enforce** | Rules apply immediately. Products that do not match are removed on the first sync. |

   If you choose **Adopt**, a **baseline import** runs automatically after setup. The collection cannot activate until the import completes — a progress indicator on the collection detail page shows its status. For most collections this finishes in seconds; large collections may take a minute or two. The collection activates itself once the import is done.

   :::tip
   If you are taking over a live collection your customers are actively browsing, use Adopt mode. You can always switch to full rule-based control later by removing the baseline.
   :::

   See [Creating & Taking Over Collections](/smart-collections/creating-and-taking-over-collections/) for the complete walkthrough of both flows.

4. **Configure membership rules**

   After choosing or creating a collection, configure the conditions that determine which products belong in it.

   ![Rule builder showing example conditions for tags and product type with a live product count](/screenshots/placeholder.png)

   A live count updates as you add conditions, showing how many products currently match. The count reflects a cached snapshot of your catalogue (refreshed every 5 minutes) — useful for checking your rules before saving, but the actual sync always uses live product data. When you are satisfied, click **Save**.

   See [Rule-Based Collections](/smart-collections/rule-based-collections/) for the full list of available conditions.

5. **Configure sort order**

   From the collection detail page, open the sort settings to choose how products should be ordered.

   The default is creation date, newest first. Options include price, title, vendor, product type, and inventory quantity — in either direction.

   You can also:

   - Pin specific products to the top or bottom of the collection
   - Enable automatic demotion of out-of-stock products to the end

   See [Sorting & Automation](/smart-collections/sorting-and-automation/) and [Product Pins](/smart-collections/product-pins/).

</Steps>

## What happens next

Once your collection is saved, Smart Collections keeps it in sync automatically. Product creates, updates, and deletes in your store trigger re-evaluation automatically. You can also trigger a manual sync at any time from the collection detail page.

To see what is happening across all your collections — active syncs in progress, recent history, and failure details — open the **Sync Dashboard** via **Sync** in the main navigation. This is the primary place to check after activation and to diagnose any issues.
