This guide walks you through installing Smart Collections and setting up your first managed collection.

## Prerequisites

- Admin access to your Shopify store
- At least one collection to manage (existing or new)

## Installation and setup

<Steps>

1. **Install the app**

   Install Smart Collections from the Shopify App Store.

   ![Smart Collections listing in the Shopify App Store with Install button visible](/screenshots/placeholder.png)

   Click **Add app**, review the permissions requested, then click **Install app**. You will be redirected to the Smart Collections dashboard.

   Smart Collections requests the **Products** permission to read your product catalogue and manage collection membership and sort order. No theme access or customer data is required.

2. **Explore the dashboard**

   The dashboard shows a summary of this month's automation activity and lists all collections currently managed by Smart Collections.

   ![Smart Collections dashboard with summary cards showing this month's automation activity](/screenshots/placeholder.png)

   When you first install, no collections are managed yet. The activity cards populate as you connect collections and syncs begin running.

3. **Create or take over a collection**

   Click **New Collection** to begin. You have two options:

   - **Create a new collection** — Smart Collections creates a new Shopify collection and manages it from the start
   - **Take over an existing collection** — Smart Collections takes over a collection already in your store; existing products are preserved while you configure rules

   ![New collection screen showing the option to create new or take over an existing collection](/screenshots/placeholder.png)

   See [Creating & Taking Over Collections](/smart-collections/creating-and-taking-over-collections/) for full details on both flows.

4. **Configure membership rules**

   After choosing or creating a collection, configure the conditions that determine which products belong in it.

   ![Rule builder showing example conditions for tags and product type with a live product count](/screenshots/placeholder.png)

   A live count updates as you add conditions, showing how many products currently match. When you are satisfied, click **Save**.

   See [Rule-Based Collections](/smart-collections/rule-based-collections/) for the full list of available conditions.

5. **Configure sort order**

   From the collection detail page, open the sort settings to choose how products should be ordered.

   The default is creation date, newest first. Options include price, title, vendor, product type, and inventory quantity — in either direction.

   You can also:

   - Pin specific products to the top or bottom of the collection
   - Enable automatic demotion of out-of-stock products to the end

   See [Sorting & Automation](/smart-collections/sorting-and-automation/) and [Product Pins](/smart-collections/product-pins/).

</Steps>

## What happens next

Once your collection is saved, Smart Collections keeps it in sync automatically. Product creates, updates, and deletes in your store trigger re-evaluation within seconds. You can also trigger a manual sync at any time from the collection detail page.
