## What's included on every plan

All plans — including Free — include the full feature set. The only difference between plans is the number of collections you can manage.

## Free trial

All paid plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to the plan's features. No charge is applied until the trial ends.

You can cancel before the trial ends with no cost.

## What counts as a managed collection

A managed collection is any Shopify collection connected to Smart Collections — either created through the app or taken over from Shopify. Collections you release or delete no longer count toward your limit.

## Reaching your collection limit

When you reach the limit for your plan, Smart Collections continues to sync all existing managed collections without interruption. You will see a prompt to upgrade when you try to connect an additional collection.

:::note
Smart Collections uses soft limits. Existing collections are never paused or stopped when you reach your plan limit — only adding new collections requires an upgrade.
:::

## Billing through Shopify

Billing is handled entirely by Shopify and charged through your existing Shopify billing cycle. No separate payment method is required.

To manage or cancel your subscription:

1. Go to **Shopify Admin → Apps**
2. Find **Smart Collections** and click **Manage subscription**

You can also manage billing from within Smart Collections under **Settings → Billing**.

Subscription changes — including trial expiry, payment failures, and plan upgrades made through Shopify — are processed automatically. Your active plan is always reflected in the app without needing to refresh.

## Changing plans

You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. If you downgrade to a lower collection limit, existing collections continue to sync — you will need to reduce your active count before adding new ones.

## Cancellation

You can cancel at any time. Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period. After cancellation:

- Smart Collections stops managing your collections
- Your Shopify collections are not deleted
- The products and sort order last set by Smart Collections remain in place
- Your Smart Collections configuration and sync history remain accessible while the app stays installed

:::caution
Cancelling your subscription is different from uninstalling the app. If you uninstall Smart Collections from Shopify, all app data — including sync history and collection configuration — is permanently deleted immediately. Uninstalling cannot be undone.
:::
