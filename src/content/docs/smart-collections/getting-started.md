This guide walks you through installing Smart Collections and setting up your first managed collection.

## Prerequisites

- Admin access to your Shopify store
- At least one collection to manage (existing or new)

## Step 1: Install the app

Install Smart Collections from the Shopify App Store.

![Smart Collections listing in the Shopify App Store with Install button visible](SCREENSHOT_PLACEHOLDER)

1. Click **Add app**
2. Review the permissions the app requests and click **Install app**
3. You will be redirected to the Smart Collections dashboard

Smart Collections requests the following permission:

- **Products** — to read your product catalogue and update collection membership and sort order

No theme changes, no code edits, and no additional permissions are required.

## Step 2: Explore the dashboard

The dashboard shows all collections currently managed by Smart Collections, along with their sync status and the last time each was updated.

![Smart Collections dashboard with summary cards showing this month's automation activity](SCREENSHOT_PLACEHOLDER)

When you first install, no collections are managed. The summary cards at the top will populate as you connect collections and syncs begin to run.

## Step 3: Create or take over a collection

Click **New Collection** to begin.

You have two options:

**Create a new collection** — Smart Collections creates a new Shopify collection and manages it from the start. Enter a title (and optionally a description and handle), then configure your rules.

**Take over an existing collection** — Smart Collections takes over management of a collection that already exists in your Shopify store. Your existing products remain in place while you configure the rules that will govern future syncs.

![New collection screen showing the option to create new or take over an existing collection](SCREENSHOT_PLACEHOLDER)

See [Creating & Taking Over Collections](/smart-collections/creating-and-taking-over-collections/) for full details on both flows.

## Step 4: Configure rules

After choosing or creating a collection, you will be taken to the rule builder. Rules determine which products belong in the collection.

![Rule builder showing example conditions for tags and product type with a live product count](SCREENSHOT_PLACEHOLDER)

As you add conditions, a live count shows how many products currently match your rules. This count updates each time you change a condition.

When you are satisfied with your rules, click **Save and sync**. Smart Collections will immediately evaluate the rules against your full product catalogue and update the collection in Shopify.

See [Rule-Based Collections](/smart-collections/rule-based-collections/) for the full list of available conditions.

## Step 5: Configure sort order

From the collection detail page, open the sort settings to choose how products should be ordered within the collection.

The default sort is by creation date, newest first. You can change this to price, title, vendor, product type, or inventory quantity — in either direction.

You can also:

- Pin specific products to the top or bottom of the collection
- Enable automatic demotion of out-of-stock products to the end of the collection

See [Sorting & Automation](/smart-collections/sorting-and-automation/) and [Product Pins](/smart-collections/product-pins/) for details.

## What happens next

Once your collection is saved, Smart Collections keeps it in sync automatically. When a product is created, updated, or deleted in your store, the affected collections are re-evaluated within seconds.

You can also trigger a manual sync at any time from the collection detail page.
