## Prerequisites

Before installing Smart Collections, confirm you have:

- A Shopify store on a supported plan
- Admin access to your Shopify store
- An active VaultifyUK Labs subscription (or free trial)

## Step 1: Install the app

Install Smart Collections from the Shopify App Store.

![Smart Collections listing in the Shopify App Store](SCREENSHOT_PLACEHOLDER)

1. Click **Add app**
2. Review the permissions requested and click **Install**
3. You will be redirected to the Smart Collections dashboard

## Step 2: Connect your store

On first launch, Smart Collections will index your product catalogue. Depending on your catalogue size this may take a few minutes.

![Initial catalogue sync progress screen](SCREENSHOT_PLACEHOLDER)

## Step 3: Create your first collection

1. In the Smart Collections dashboard, click **New Collection**
2. Choose whether to create a new Shopify collection or take over management of an existing one
3. Configure your rules
4. Click **Save and sync**

![New collection setup screen with rule builder](SCREENSHOT_PLACEHOLDER)
