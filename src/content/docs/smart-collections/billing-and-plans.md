## Free trial

Smart Collections includes a free trial period so you can evaluate the app before committing to a paid plan. During the trial all features are available without restriction.

## Billing through Shopify

Smart Collections billing is handled entirely by Shopify. You are charged through your existing Shopify billing cycle. No separate payment method is required.

To manage or cancel your subscription:

1. Go to your Shopify Admin → **Apps**
2. Find **Smart Collections**
3. Click **Manage subscription**

Alternatively, you can manage billing from within the Smart Collections app under **Settings → Billing**.

## What happens when a plan limit is reached?

If you exceed the limit for your plan (for example, you try to create more collections than your plan allows), Smart Collections will prompt you to upgrade. Existing collections continue to sync unaffected.

## Cancellation

You can cancel at any time. Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period. After cancellation, Smart Collections stops managing your collections but does not delete them from Shopify.
