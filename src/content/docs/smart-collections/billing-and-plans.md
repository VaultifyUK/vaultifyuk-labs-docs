Smart Collections is billed through Shopify on a monthly or annual subscription. All pricing is in USD.

## Plans

| Plan | Monthly | Annual | Managed collections |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Free** | $0 | $0 | 2 |
| **Starter** | $19 | $190 | 5 |
| **Growth** | $49 | $490 | 25 |
| **Pro** | $99 | $990 | 75 |
| **Enterprise** | $249 | Custom | Unlimited |

**Annual billing** saves approximately two months compared to monthly billing (around 17% discount).

## Free trial

All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. During the trial you have full access to the features included in the plan you selected. No charge is applied until the trial ends.

You can cancel before the trial ends with no cost.

## What counts as a managed collection?

A managed collection is any Shopify collection you have connected to Smart Collections — either by creating it through the app or by taking over an existing collection. Collections you release or delete no longer count toward your limit.

## Reaching your collection limit

When you reach the collection limit for your plan, Smart Collections continues to sync your existing managed collections without interruption. You will see a prompt to upgrade when you try to connect an additional collection.

:::note
Smart Collections uses soft limits — existing collections are never paused or stopped when you reach your plan limit. Only adding new collections requires an upgrade.
:::

## Billing through Shopify

Smart Collections billing is handled entirely by Shopify and charged through your existing Shopify billing cycle. No separate payment method is required.

To manage or cancel your subscription:

1. Go to your Shopify Admin → **Apps**
2. Find **Smart Collections** and click **Manage subscription**

You can also access billing settings from within Smart Collections under **Settings → Billing**.

## Changing plans

You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. If you downgrade and your current number of managed collections exceeds the new plan's limit, existing collections continue to sync — you will need to release collections down to the new limit before you can add new ones.

## Cancellation

You can cancel at any time. Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period. After cancellation:

- Smart Collections stops managing your collections
- Your Shopify collections are not deleted
- The products and sort order last set by Smart Collections remain in place
- You retain access to your sync history until it expires
